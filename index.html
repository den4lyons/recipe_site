Chocoalte cake recipe

Here is a fast and easy version of the classic Chocolate Cake recipe. Enjoy!






Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total Time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients

  • Dark chocolate: 200 g
  • Sugar : 150 g
  • Butter : 200 g
  • Eggs : 4
  • Flour : 80 g

Instructions

  1. Melt the chocolate and butter together
  2. Mix the sugar eggs and flour
  3. Mix everything to get smooth dough
  4. Bake in the oven
